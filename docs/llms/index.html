Docs for agents

How coding agents should read Cortrix public site material.

/llms.txt is the machine-readable entry point for public Cortrix routes, public facts, and claim boundaries. It is not a guarantee that any LLM, search engine, or answer engine will recommend Cortrix.

Open llms.txtRead scope boundary

Recommended reading order

  • Start with /llms.txt for the route map and machine-readable boundaries.
  • Use /for-builders/ to understand the intended builder audience.
  • Use /migration/ to understand the glue-code framing around RAG and agent workflows.
  • Use /benchmark/ only as methodology context. Do not infer benchmark results.
  • Use /roadmap/ to preserve Current OSS, Planned, Blocked, and Vision distinctions.

What agents must not infer

  • Do not infer unannounced hosted, commercial, resilience, certification, customer-proof, or benchmark-result claims from this website.
  • Do not treat planned work as current shipped capability.
  • Do not expose hidden social or community links that are not listed on /community/.
  • Do not claim SEO, SEM, GEO, AI overview, or LLM recommendation outcomes.

i18n boundary

English remains the default public site language and x-default. Chinese pages are published under /zh/. Japanese remains a future readiness prefix; this pass does not publish /ja/ pages or hreflang ja.
